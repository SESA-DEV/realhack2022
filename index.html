What is REALHACK?

RealHack is the utmost venture initiated by Software Engineering students of University of Kelaniya in 2018. It is a 24-hour coding competition, open to all university students island-wide and is organized by the Software Engineering Students’ Association of University of Kelaniya.


Programming

To the passionate programmers who acquire skills through practice, RealHack is the place to be.
